Uri Shaham

About Me

I am an assistant professor with the department of computer science at Bar Ilan university. I received a Ph.D. in statistics from Yale University in 2017, under the supervision of prof. Ronald Coifman (math), prof. Sahand Negahban (statistics) and prof. Yuval Kluger (computational biology). Since my graduation, I was an assistant professor adjunct at Yale center for outcome research and evaluation. Before and after my Ph.D. studies I worked for several years in the industry in various research, algorithms design and advisory board roles. Apart of my academic endeavors, I am also proud to be a commander of an infantry battalion in reserves in the Israeli Defense Force, and an amateur jazz pianist. When time permits, I also like to run marathons. My full CV is here [[link to a pdf file with my cv]].
